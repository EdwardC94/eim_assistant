    The Travel Protection provides coverage for cancellation or interruption of your trip due to an unforeseen emergency reason; either an emergency medical situation with yourself or a covered family member, or one of the specific emergency reasons for cancellation as outlined in the terms that govern the policy.

    You can initiate a claim by visiting our website {{$parent.data.message.closing.urlForClaims}}, under the option “File a Claim” and the provide to the system the information necessary for the claim, shortly after you will receive an email with your claim number and the forms needed to adjudicate your request, along with the list of documents needed for your specific type of claim. If you will be filing the claim due to a medical reason you will be required to supply a completed and signed Physician Statement form that you will be able to get over the website as well

    Once you have fully completed and signed the claim forms you can submit them along with the rest of the proper documentation and any other information you consider relevant for the case via:
