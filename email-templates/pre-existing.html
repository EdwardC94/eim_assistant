Any injury occurring prior to and including the effective date of insurance; or, any illness occurring 120 days prior to the purchase of the contract including the effective date of insurance for which treatment by a licensed physician has been sought or advised, or for which symptoms exist which would cause a prudent person to seek diagnosis, care or treatment.
